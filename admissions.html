Admission Process

Simple steps to begin your journey with us

1

Choose Your Course

Review our course offerings and select the program that aligns with your career goals. Visit our Courses page to explore detailed information about each program, including duration, syllabus, and fee structure.

2

Submit Online Application

Complete the online application form below with accurate information. Ensure all required documents are ready for upload, including academic transcripts, citizenship certificate, and passport-sized photographs.

3

Document Verification

Our admissions team will review your application and documents within 3-5 business days. You will receive an email confirmation once your documents are verified.

4

Entrance Assessment (if applicable)

For certain programs, you may be required to appear for an entrance assessment or interview. You will be notified about the date, time, and format of the assessment.

5

Admission Confirmation

Upon successful completion of the assessment, you will receive an admission letter. Complete the enrollment process by paying the course fee and submitting any remaining documents.
