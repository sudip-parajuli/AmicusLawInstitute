Our History

Founded with a vision to revolutionize legal education in Nepal, Amicus Institute of Law emerged from the collective dream of experienced legal professionals, educators, and advocates who recognized the need for a comprehensive, practical approach to legal training.

Established in Kathmandu, the heart of Nepal's judicial system, our institute was built on the principle that future lawyers need more than theoretical knowledge‚Äîthey need practical skills, ethical grounding, and a deep understanding of both Nepali and international legal systems.

Since our inception, we have been committed to maintaining the highest standards of academic excellence while ensuring our programs remain accessible to talented students from all backgrounds. Our curriculum has been carefully designed in consultation with Supreme Court advocates, legal scholars, and bar council members to ensure it meets the evolving needs of Nepal's legal profession.
